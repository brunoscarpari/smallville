THE SERIE

Smallville follows the coming-of-age adventures of teenage Clark Kent in his fictional hometown of Smallville, Kansas, before he formally becomes the Man of Steel. The first four seasons focus on the high school life of Clark and his friends, his complicated romance with neighbor girl Lana Lang, and his friendship with future nemesis Lex Luthor

Action-adventure, Superhero
Created by Alfred Gough, Miles Millar
Starring: Tom Welling, Kristen Kreuk, Michael Rosenbaum
